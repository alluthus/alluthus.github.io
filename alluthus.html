second album

although memory lane was thought to be the first and final album released, alluthus released his second EP Undertow mere months after. with many criticizing the usage of samples/loops on both releases, this self-titled album uses them to the max. tracks from the undertow EP such as "24/7" and "Press Forward" are included on this album, along with new tracks.
