The first album by Alluthus

In late 2017, Alluthus (then using the alias Verso for music) planned out a debut album but it fell through in early 2018. It wasn't until late 2019 when he returned to recording tracks. The lead single, Aura, was released in January 2020. The second single, Lost, was included in his debut EP, Golden Days. The third single, Persona, was released in April 2020.
