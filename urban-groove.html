Debut single

Released in late 2017 as Alluthus' debut single, Urban Groove was released to SoundCloud. The track originally was the lead single for an upcoming album by Alluthus called "Infinite", which was scrapped in early 2018.

Urban Groove was included in Alluthus' debut EP, "Golden Days" in March 2020 and later re-released to stores as a standalone single in June 2020.
