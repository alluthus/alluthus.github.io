About Alluthus

Indie artist Alluthus had always wanted to create music since he was a young child. Having very little knowledge on playing instruments did not stop him from producing tracks on GarageBand.

Alluthus is the alias used by Joseph "Joey" Madril III, a Las Vegas, Nevada native. A self-taught photographer, web designer and graphic designer, Alluthus' music is based on tons of different genres, from country to hard rock and electronic.

"Growing up, I listened to a lot of different musicial genres such as country and punk pop. A lot of the artists I listened to as a child inspired what I now make."

In 2017, Alluthus shared his first track, Urban Groove, on SoundCloud using the alias "Verso". Plans for an independent album fell through though by 2018.

"I wanted to improve on my skills in graphic design and also at the time, I wanted to learn web development."

In 2018, Alluthus joined the STiBaRC Team, a small group of teenagers who manage STiBaRC. In late 2019, indie record label STiBaRC Records was founded by Alluthus, Joshua Herron and Ben Border. Alluthus' first single in major stores was released to stores in January 2020 but was later taken down a few days after.

"I used 'Verso' as the artist name for my first single on the stores, and that was a mistake. I didn't know there were so many artists with the same name! My music got mixed in with other artists on so many different services. After that, I decided to use 'Alluthus' for my future releases."

In March 2020, Alluthus' first digital EP, Golden Days, appeared in stores. In April 2020, his second single, Persona, was released to stores.

"Although I'm releasing music to stores using the name 'Alluthus', I still use 'Verso' for my designs. I don't regret using 'Alluthus' for my releases though because it can be found easily online unlike 'Verso'."
