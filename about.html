about joseph

joseph "joey" madril iii is a self-taught web designer, graphic designer and photographer. madril started making content online in 2015 after a friend introduced him to scratch, a website that teaches the basics of programming. after many failed attemps to make a game called life and an operating system simulator (fiber nova) with a group of teens around his age, madril moved on to learn the basics of web design and further improve his novice skills in graphic design.

despite having support from family and friends, madril felt as if the content he made simply was not good enough. madril has attempted to make other projects online—from third party clients of a friend's social media service, STiBaRC, to a web comic. in 2020, he released two instrumental ep's and an album consisting of many samples under the STiBaRC Records label he created with joshua herron and ben border.

tired of creating content exclusively to appease others, madril decided to create things he himself would enjoy.

when not on the computer, madril enjoys reading, writing, taking photos, and sleeping.
