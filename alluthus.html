second album

Following Memory Lane (2020), this 18 track self-titled album features brand new and previously released tracks found on the Undertow EP. Sarcastic, witty remarks blended with instrumental beats and pre-recorded loops, Alluthus blends various genres like its predecessor. If monotone vocals and mediocre mixing is your thing, this album is the perfect listen.
