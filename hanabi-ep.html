a looptastic EP of various genre

Joseph Madril's third EP–the HANABI EP–explores chiptune sounds and further delves into the dance-pop genre found on his 2022 self-titled album. Created while Madril was in Japan, the literal meaning of "hanabi" in English translates to "fireworks". With minimum production on songs such as "Seppen (Snowflakes)" and "Ai no uta (Song of love)", the HANABI EP closes off strongly with the 2021 single "FLARE", which returns back to Madril's original rock edge.
